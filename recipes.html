loco cream pie

what you will need:

  • meringue or whipped cream
  • cherry
  • 1 and 3/4 Tbsp. cocoa
  • 4 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 lg. can evaporated milk
  • 1 1/2 c. milk
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 4 egg yolks
  • dash of salt
  • 3 Tbsp. butter

How to make the pie:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients, add eggs and milk.
  2. Cook until thick, add butter and vanilla.
  3. Cool.
  4. Pour into two baked pie shells.
  5. Top with meringue or whipped cream.

The cherry tree told them that their homes were East and gave them each a magic cherry. They got home and baked the cherries. The cherry then turned into pie, for they were all poor and these pies gave them food. The end.
